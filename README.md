This project presents a comprehensive research study on the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in modern cyber warfare and cybersecurity operations. The research explores how AI and Machine Learning (ML) technologies are transforming cyber defense mechanisms, threat intelligence, attack detection, and predictive cybersecurity strategies.
The study examines the growing role of AI in both defensive and offensive cyber operations, including autonomous threat detection, cyber espionage, critical infrastructure protection, and AI-enabled attack techniques. Through an extensive review of contemporary research, the project identifies current challenges, emerging trends, and future directions in AI-driven cyber warfare.
Objectives
  Analyze the role of AI in modern cyber warfare environments.
  Study machine learning approaches used in cyber threat detection and classification.
  Evaluate predictive techniques for forecasting cyber attacks against critical infrastructure.
  Examine the impact of AI on cyber defense, cyber resilience, and national security.
  Identify research gaps and future opportunities in AI-enabled cybersecurity systems
